# Keith Drury - Complete Collection of Articles & Writings

**Keith Wesley Drury** (July 28, 1945 - April 7, 2024) was a pastor, denominational leader, author, and Professor of Theology and Ministry at Indiana Wesleyan University. He wrote the famous "Tuesday Column" from 1995-2012 and authored over a dozen books. Below is every article and writing discovered online.
